2)What does fast-forward in git mean? 
Ans:Fast-forward merges forward the beginning of your main branch to the conclusion of your feature branch. This maintains the chronological order of all contributions made there while seamlessly integrating your feature branch back into your main branch.

3)Why should you have different branches? 
Ans: In general, branches are made to lessen the amount of modification requests to the main branch. Prior to updating the main branch, we first make changes to the child branches.
